Not Just an
Ordinary Love Story

In the heart of modern times, where tradition and love intertwine, destiny wove its magical threads through the story of us. It began with a simple request from our families to connect over the phone, to see if there was any spark of understanding between us. Little did we know, that first video call would ignite a flame that would illuminate our lives forever.

As her face appeared on my screen, adorned with grace and adorned with the most captivating eyes I had ever beheld, my heart skipped a beat. Her beauty was beyond compare, and as we delved into conversation, it felt as though time itself had slowed to savor our words.

For hours, we exchanged not just pleasantries, but pieces of our souls. Our ideologies intertwined seamlessly, our tastes and interests aligning as if guided by destiny. It was an organic dance of hearts, a symphony of connection that left us both breathless.

Then came the day when I journeyed to her home, anticipation coursing through my veins like a river eager to meet the sea. And there she was, descending the stairs in a saree, her ethereal presence casting a spell upon me. In that moment, she was not just beautiful; she was the very essence of divinity, an angel gracing the mortal realm.

As our eyes met, it was as if the universe whispered its approval, weaving our destinies together in a tapestry of love. With certainty in our hearts, we uttered the words that would seal our bond, saying "yes" to a future entwined in love and commitment.

And now, as the date of our union draws near, I cannot help but marvel at the serendipity that brought us together. On the auspicious day of August 17th, amidst the blessings of family and the whispers of romance, we shall embark on a journey of forever, hand in hand, soul to soul.

“To the world you are one person, but to one person you are the world”.
